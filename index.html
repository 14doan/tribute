Jobs was an abandoned child. Blessed to be adopted to loving parents. His father, a carpenter, taught him to pay attention to details that nobody sees. To also beautify the inner structure of a product. This lead to him to explore for his own inner mechanism. So he experienced with LSD and went on a spiritual journey to India. When asked about what he learned there, he replied "intuition". Intuitively, he knew he didn't have much time on earth.
Jobs is a rebel who had the vision to change the world. He didn't hesistate to be harsh and straightforward with others. For he wanted to get the most out of his team, he pushed them beyond their limits in order to bring his imagination to life. Products that would shake the world and change how billions of people interact with their devices.

Top 3 Achievements:

  1. Creating a device with his friend that gave him free access to all phone lines
  2. Bringing touchscreen display onto phones
  3. Managing Pixar and helped produced classic movies like Toy Story
